ABOUT LITTLE ANGEL

  • We shall strive for constant refinement and improvement of the quality of education provided.
  • The only really substantial thing education can do is help us to become continuous lifelong learners.
  • We hope that students in the classroom of tomorrow will be encouraged to be creative, not conforming and learn to cooperate rather than compete.
  • Expectations are set high for our new school. We aim for all children to be inspired and motivated to achieve their full potential and for our school community to become lifelong learners.
